# Capture Back: Team Member Onboarding Lesson

This note captures the onboarding lesson from considering how a new collaborator would enter a Workbench.

The key insight:

```text
A Workbench should onboard people into a workflow, not just expose a repo.
```

The first newcomer action should usually be a reaction note, not a change.

This tests whether the repo makes the Workbench promise legible:

```text
durable continuity
human custody
LLM reasoning support
Capture Back into a governed repo
small verified steps
```

A team member onboarding simulation is a reusable Workbench Loop.
