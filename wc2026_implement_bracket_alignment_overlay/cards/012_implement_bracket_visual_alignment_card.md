# Card 012 — Implement Bracket Visual Alignment

## Intent

Implement the bracket visual alignment rule in the static HTML.

## Acceptance

- Round of 16 slots align between their source Round of 32 matches.
- Quarterfinal slots align between source Round of 16 slots.
- Semifinal slots align between source Quarterfinal slots.
- The final area aligns from semifinal sources.
- Later-round slots have labels/tooltips.
- `index.html` opens locally.
