# Narrow Left Team Well Rule

## Purpose

The left group/team well should be narrow so the bracket has more horizontal room.

## Rule

The team well should be roughly half the previous width.

Group cards and team rows should be compact:

- narrow left column
- single-column team list
- flag + short team name
- hidden secondary identifiers
- tooltip for full identity

## Data preservation

Do not remove team names, groups, abbreviations, or source data.

This is a public layout change only.
