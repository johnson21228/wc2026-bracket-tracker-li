# Capture Back: Not-Joined Startup Guidance

## Intent

When the site loads and the player has not joined the pool, show a similarly timed player-facing guidance message.

## Runtime copy

Playing Bracketeering requires you to join the pool. Tap the button with the person icon to join. Tap the button with the “i” to get information about playing the game.

## Joined player copy

Your picks have been loaded.

## Boundary

This is copy/status behavior only. It does not change Supabase auth, joining, local draft behavior, pick storage, scoring, official truth, or standings.

The message avoids technical storage terms and tells the player where to join and where to find game information.
