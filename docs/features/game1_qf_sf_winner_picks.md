# Game 1 QF/SF Winner Picks

Game 1 can continue as the long-lived bracket workspace after R32 assignment. Once R16 winners are chosen, QF slots may be tapped to choose between the two R16 winners that feed that QF slot. Once QF winners are chosen, SF slots may be tapped to choose between the two QF winners that feed that SF slot.

This follows the same resolved-source-slot rule introduced for R16, but one round deeper at a time:

- R16 choices come from two assigned R32 slots.
- QF choices come from two resolved R16 winner slots.
- SF choices come from two resolved QF winner slots.

The runtime must not show group-eligibility pools for QF or SF. Those menus must show only the two teams that have actually advanced into that match according to the user's bracket picks.
