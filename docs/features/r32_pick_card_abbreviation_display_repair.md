# R32 Pick Card Abbreviation Display Repair

This repair enforces the compact R32 pick-card rule in Game 1.

The board-defined slot remains the geometry authority. The compact filled card should use the large flag plus the three-letter abbreviation only. Full team names are reserved for tooltip/details surfaces.

## Acceptance

- Filled R32 pick cards show a flag and a three-letter code such as `KOR`, `MEX`, or `USA`.
- Filled R32 pick cards do not show full team names such as `South Korea` or `Korea Republic` on the card face.
- All 48 teams have three-letter abbreviation data.
- Pick persistence and chooser behavior remain unchanged.
