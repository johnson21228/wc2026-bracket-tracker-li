# Player Join Experience

Bracketeering should feel like joining a bracket game, not configuring an account.

## Join paths

The preferred join order is:

1. Continue with Google
2. Email me a sign-in link
3. Play locally on this browser

## First-visit copy

The join panel should use simple player-facing copy:

- Join Bracketeering
- Continue with Google
- Email me a sign-in link
- Play locally on this browser

## Signed-in copy

After sign-in, the board should show simple save-state language:

- Signed in
- Picks will be saved
- Welcome back
- Loaded your bracket

## Local-play copy

Local play should explain its boundary clearly:

No account needed.
Your picks are saved only on this browser.
Use Google if you want saved play across visits/devices.

## Experience invariant

The player should experience the main path as:

Open link → Continue with Google → return to board → make picks

The board interaction remains:

Click a slot → choose a team → pick advances → board updates
