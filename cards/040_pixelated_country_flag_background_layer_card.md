# Card 040 — Pixelated country flag background layer

## Intent

Place a country-flag background behind the Game 1 playfield using one of the country flags already present in the page data.

## Requirements

- The background must sit behind the board and hit targets.
- The selected flag must come from the in-page team data.
- The image must be scaled up and rendered with a pixelated/crisp look.
- The background must be transparent enough not to distract from the game.
- The user can select a different country from the page controls.
- The page opens after apply for immediate review.
