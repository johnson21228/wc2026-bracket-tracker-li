# Card 007 — Add YouTube Highlights To Match Cards

## Intent

Add optional YouTube highlight metadata and UI to each match card.

## Acceptance

- Each match can store a YouTube highlight URL.
- The site extracts or stores a YouTube video id.
- The match card displays a highlight link or embed when present.
- Missing highlights show a clear placeholder.
- Highlights do not affect results, standings, or scoring.
- Adding or changing a highlight is captured back.
