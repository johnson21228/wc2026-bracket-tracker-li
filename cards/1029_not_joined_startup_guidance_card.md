# Card 1029: Not-Joined Startup Guidance

## Goal

Show a player-facing startup message when the site loads and the player is not joined.

## Acceptance

- Joined players see simple loaded-picks copy.
- Not-joined players see guidance to join the pool.
- The message points to the person icon and the info button.
- No localStorage/Supabase/local draft language appears in the player message.
