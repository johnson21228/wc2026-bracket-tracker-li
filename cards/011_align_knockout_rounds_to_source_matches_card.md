# Card 011 — Align Knockout Rounds To Source Matches

## Intent

Improve the static bracket layout so later-round slots are vertically aligned with their source matches.

## Acceptance

- Round of 16 slots align with their two Round of 32 source matches.
- Quarterfinal slots align with their two Round of 16 source slots.
- Semifinal slots align with their two Quarterfinal source slots.
- Final slots align with semifinal sources.
- The layout remains screenshot-friendly.
- Source mappings are represented in data or code clearly enough to preserve alignment logic.
