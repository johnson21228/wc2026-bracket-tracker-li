# Card 146 — Anchor Choice Menu to Picked Item

## Intent
A choice menu must appear adjacent to the bracket item that opened it. The menu belongs to the picked slot, not to a generic viewport location.

## Acceptance
- Tapping/clicking a pickable bracket slot opens the menu next to that slot.
- Board scrolling keeps the menu attached to the board surface.
- The menu moves with the board and does not close on scroll.
- Existing selection persistence still works.
- Long menus can still scroll internally.
