# Card 006 — Capture Current Solution Against Living Intent

## Intent

Add the product-level principle that the Workbench presents the current solution against LI-stated goals.

## Why

The World Cup tracker shows that continuity is not the final deliverable.

Continuity exists to keep the solution current as facts, goals, and use change.

## Acceptance

- LI includes a current-solution-against-living-intent rule.
- Product docs include the EWOR pitch refinement.
- The World Cup tracker is recorded as a proof case:
  `poster → data → static HTML → hosted pool → scoring system`.
