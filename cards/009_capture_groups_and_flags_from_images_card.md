# Card 009 — Capture Groups And Flags From Images

## Intent

Preserve the uploaded group/flag images and derive normalized group/team data from them.

## Acceptance

- Source images are preserved.
- Group data is stored in `data/groups_from_flags_images.json`.
- Team data is stored in `data/teams_from_flags_images.json`.
- Naming differences are recorded.
- Site requirement is captured: Groups section with flags and team names.
- Data is marked image-derived and pending official verification.
