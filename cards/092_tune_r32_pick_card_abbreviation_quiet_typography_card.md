# Card 092 — Tune R32 Pick Card Abbreviation Quiet Typography

## Intent

Tone down the R32 pick-card abbreviation typography so the compact card reads as a quiet board label.

## User-visible behavior

- The compact R32 pick card still shows flag + three-letter team abbreviation.
- The abbreviation is no longer bold/heavy/headline-like.
- The same typography is used for every R32 pick card.
- Full team names remain in the tooltip/details surface.

## Guardrail

This is a visual-only card-face change. It must not alter pick storage, team eligibility, slot mapping, or tooltip data authority.
