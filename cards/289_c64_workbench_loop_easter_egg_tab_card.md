# Card 289 — C64 Workbench Loop Easter Egg Tab

## Intent

Add a third Workbench Easter egg panel tab that explains the C64 Workbench Loop.

## Product decision

The Bracketeering Easter egg panel can carry a reusable Workbench story beyond Bracketeering itself. The new tab shows the C64 Workbench Stage 0 Learning Loop image and explanatory copy.

## Acceptance

- The Easter egg panel includes a `C64 Loop` tab.
- The tab renders the C64 Workbench image.
- The tab includes the supplied explanatory copy.
- Existing Story and Workbench tabs remain available.
- The image is stored as a repo asset, not hotlinked.
