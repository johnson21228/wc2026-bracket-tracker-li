# Card 018 — Limit Drag/Drop To Round Of 32

## Intent

Update the static HTML so teams can only be dragged and dropped into Round-of-32 slots.

## Acceptance

- R32 slots accept drag/drop.
- R16, QF, SF, Final, and Champion slots do not accept team drops.
- Later-round slots can still display labels/tooltips.
- Export/import continues to work.
- Existing invalid later-round direct drops should not be encouraged.
