# Evaluate Workbench Against Existing Tools

You are evaluating a Workbench LI repo against existing industry patterns.

Compare it with:

- RAG
- GraphRAG
- enterprise knowledge graphs
- AI memory systems
- CRM systems
- knowledge management systems
- personal knowledge management systems
- document management systems
- AI workbenches
- agentic AI platforms
- human-in-the-loop governance frameworks

For each comparison, identify:

1. what already exists
2. what is different terminology for existing ideas
3. what may be genuinely different
4. what requires evidence
5. what is useful but not unique
6. what claims should be softened

Do not assume novelty.

Focus on clarity, evidence, and responsible positioning.
