# Tune R32 Pick Card Abbreviation Display

Use this prompt when the R32 pick card face becomes crowded or inconsistent.

The compact R32 pick card should show only a large flag and the selected team's three-letter abbreviation. The full team name and extended slot/rule context should appear in one tooltip/details surface.

Do not change pick identity, slot ids, eligibility rules, localStorage schema, or menu behavior while tuning the compact card rendering.
