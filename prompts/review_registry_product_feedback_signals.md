# Review Registry Product Feedback Signals

Search registered Workbenches for `data/registry/*product_feedback_signal*.json` and summarize product-level lessons.

For each signal, identify:

- source Workbench;
- observed failure or opportunity;
- accepted lesson;
- recommended Workbench Product update;
- likely Registry dashboard change;
- whether the lesson should become template LI.

Then propose a Workbench Product Builder update plan.
