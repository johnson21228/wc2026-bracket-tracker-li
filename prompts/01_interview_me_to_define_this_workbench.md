# Interview Me to Define This Workbench

Ask me a small number of questions that will help define this Workbench LI repo.

Focus on:

- the person or team the workbench serves
- the work that needs continuity
- the decisions that need to be remembered
- the source material that should govern the work
- the outputs the workbench should help produce
- the risks of losing context in ordinary chat

After the interview, summarize the proposed workbench in plain language and suggest a minimal first repo structure.
