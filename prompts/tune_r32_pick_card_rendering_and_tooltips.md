# Tune R32 Pick Card Rendering and Tooltips

Patch the WC2026 site so filled R32 pick cards render as team-first cards.

Requirements:

- Larger flag in filled card.
- Team name is the dominant text.
- Slot/rule text is hidden or quiet secondary metadata.
- Filled card covers competing slot labels underneath it.
- Hover/focus tooltip exposes extended properties: team, group, slot rule, qualification path, and edit hint.
- Clicking/tapping a filled Game 1 pick card should reopen the chooser for that slot.
