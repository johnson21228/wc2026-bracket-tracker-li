# Workbench LI Spine

## Purpose

This repository exists to help an individual or team create and grow a Workbench LI repo: a durable, repo-backed place for AI-assisted reasoning, human judgment, validation, history, and handoff.

## Governing idea

Giving humans tools to govern at human scale in an AI future.

## Boundary

The LLM may assist with reasoning, drafting, structuring, comparison, critique, and planning.

The repository remains the custody layer for continuity: source context, decisions, validation evidence, prompts, cards, generated artifacts, history, and future handoff.

## First outcome

The first useful outcome is not a perfect repo. The first useful outcome is a coherent starting workbench that can be improved through use.
