# Two-Game Pool Model Rule

## Game 1 — Round-of-32 Qualifier Pick Game

Before the official Round of 32 field is known, each player picks 32 teams they believe will advance from the 48-team group stage.

Game 1 does not require bracket slots.

## Game 2 — Full Knockout Bracket Pick Game

After the official Round of 32 field and bracket slots are known, each player fills out the entire knockout bracket:

- Round of 32
- Round of 16
- Quarterfinal
- Semifinal
- Final
- Champion

## Timing rule

Do not open Game 2 until the official Round of 32 is known.

## Lock rule

Game 1 and Game 2 have separate lock dates.

Post-lock corrections must be recorded as corrections.
