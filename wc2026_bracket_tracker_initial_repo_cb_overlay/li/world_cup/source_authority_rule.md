# Source Authority Rule

## Purpose

This rule governs how tournament facts enter the Workbench.

## Source priority

1. FIFA official tournament pages
2. official federation or broadcaster sources
3. reputable sports data/news sources
4. user-provided manual override
5. poster-derived or screenshot-derived starter data

## Poster-derived data

The uploaded schedule poster is preserved as a source artifact, but it is not the final authority.

Poster-derived data may seed the Workbench, but must be verified or corrected against official sources.

## No silent changes

Never silently change:

- teams
- group assignments
- match dates
- results
- standings
- official Round of 32
- player picks
- scoring rules
