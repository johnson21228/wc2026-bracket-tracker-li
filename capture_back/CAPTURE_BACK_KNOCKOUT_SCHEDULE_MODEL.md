# Capture Back — Knockout Schedule Model and Pub Image Authority

This CB adds structured knockout schedule truth and updates the pub background image authority.

The key governance decision is that generated pub art may support the mood of the app, but the schedule text in the image is not trusted as data. The JSON model is the schedule authority.

The uploaded/generated knockout pub image had an incorrect chalkboard note saying `24 TEAMS`. This CB corrects the runtime/source image to communicate `32 TEAMS`.
