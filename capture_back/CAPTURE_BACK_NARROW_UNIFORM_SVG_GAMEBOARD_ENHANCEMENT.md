# Capture Back — Narrow Uniform SVG Gameboard Enhancement

## Captured idea
The uniform SVG gameboard may benefit from a slightly narrower geometry. This should be preserved as a possible enhancement, not applied immediately.

## Reason
The browser review shows the board working, but the total width may be wider than ideal. Reducing slot widths and column spacing could make the board feel more contained without losing the full bracket structure.

## LI boundary
A narrower board should be generated from the SVG geometry model and projected into the SVG, PNG, and manifest artifacts together.

CSS-only narrowing is not sufficient because it can drift from hit target and pick-card geometry.
