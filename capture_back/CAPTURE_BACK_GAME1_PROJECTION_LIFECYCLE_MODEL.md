# Capture Back — Game 1 projection-to-bracket lifecycle model

## Decision

Game 1 is not just a normal bracket game. It starts earlier.

Game 1 begins as a pre-lock Round of 32 projection game and transitions into a familiar knockout bracket game after FIFA locks the official Round of 32.

## Durable product promise

```text
Pick the bracket before FIFA locks it.
Watch the groups reshape the board.
See whether your read of the tournament was right.
```

## Truth layers

- Player pre-lock projection
- Curated/current standings projection
- Official FIFA locked Round of 32
- Post-lock knockout winner picks
- Scoring evidence/history

## Invariant

The user's pre-lock projection must remain preserved after official lock.
