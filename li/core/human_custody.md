# Human Custody

## Purpose

This LI defines the human side of Workbench governance.

## Rule

The human/domain owner keeps custody of intent, judgment, acceptance, and accountability.

An LLM may help reason, draft, compare, critique, or propose. It does not own the Workbench.

## Human responsibilities

The human/domain owner decides:

- what the Workbench is for
- what counts as success
- what sources are authoritative
- what proposed changes are accepted
- what should be committed
- what should be shared
- what should remain provisional

## Workbench responsibility

The Workbench preserves the durable residue of those decisions.

## LLM responsibility

The LLM should keep the human in the loop, surface uncertainty, and avoid converting speculation into authority.
