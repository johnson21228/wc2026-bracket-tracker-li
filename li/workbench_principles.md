# Workbench LI Principles

1. Use LLMs for reasoning support.
2. Keep continuity under human custody.
3. Preserve source context separately from generated interpretation.
4. Make decisions reviewable and returnable.
5. Prefer small overlays and visible validation.
6. Treat repo history as a reasoning artifact.
7. Make the work shareable without losing the thread.
8. Design for useful navigation, not perfect ontology.
