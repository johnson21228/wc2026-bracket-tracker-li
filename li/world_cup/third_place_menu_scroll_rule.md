# LI Rule — Long third-place menu scrollability

A long candidate menu is part of the interaction surface, not a page artifact.

The menu must be independently scrollable when the pointer or touch is over it. Board-scroll dismissal may close menus only when the scroll gesture originates outside the choice menu.

This rule protects reviewability: a reviewer must be able to inspect and select any eligible third-place team from a long menu on desktop, iPad, or iPhone.
