# Game 1 Pub Background Image Rule

A Game 1 decorative background may be used when it improves the game-board feeling without interfering with selection.

Rules:

- The background image must be under `site/assets/playfield/`.
- The layer must be beneath the board template and hotspots.
- The layer must have `pointer-events: none`.
- The opacity must be tweakable through a CSS custom property or simple UI control.
- The background must not become a source of match truth; it is visual atmosphere only.
