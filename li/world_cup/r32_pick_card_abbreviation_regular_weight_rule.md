# R32 Pick Card Abbreviation Regular Weight Rule

The compact R32 pick card face is a board-slot occupant card. It must show the selected team's flag and three-letter team abbreviation using calm, regular-weight typography.

## Rule

- The R32 pick card face must use the team abbreviation, not the full team name.
- The R32 pick card abbreviation must not be bold, extra-bold, black, or heavy.
- The R32 pick card abbreviation should use a regular or medium weight so it reads as a label, not a headline.
- The same font size, line height, font family, and font weight must be used for every R32 pick card abbreviation.
- The flag may remain visually prominent within the slot height.
- The full team name belongs in the tooltip/details surface, not the compact card face.

## Target

Use one shared abbreviation metric set:

- font-size: 24px or slot-derived equivalent
- font-weight: 400 or 500
- line-height: 1
- no text-transform changes beyond uppercase abbreviation data

## Boundary

Typography changes are visual only. They must not change pick state, slot id, slot rule, team abbreviation data, tooltip data, menu eligibility, or hit testing.
