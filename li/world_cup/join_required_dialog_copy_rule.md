# Join Required Dialog Copy Rule

The Join/Profile dialog must treat joining the Pool as required for Bracketeering play.

Signed-out players must see:

- `Join the Pool`
- `Playing Bracketeering requires you to join the Pool.`
- `Use Google sign-in to avoid email verification. If you use email, check your spam folder if the sign-in link does not appear in your inbox.`
- `Not joined yet.`

Signed-in players must see the Profile posture and only need support to edit their player name and log out:

- `Profile`
- `Edit your player name or log out.`
- `Edit your player name below, or log out.`
- `Joined status: Joined`

The signed-out dialog must not say players can explore before joining, keep playing locally, or that no account is needed for local play.

The signed-in Profile dialog must not carry saved-picks loaded status. Saved-picks loaded status belongs to the joined-picks status surface.
