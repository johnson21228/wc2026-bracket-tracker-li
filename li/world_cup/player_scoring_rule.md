# Player Scoring Rule

## Game 1 proposed scoring

Default:

- 1 point for each correctly predicted advancing Round-of-32 team
- optional perfect-32 bonus, unset by default

## Game 2 proposed scoring

Default:

- Round of 32 correct winner: 1 point
- Round of 16 correct winner: 2 points
- Quarterfinal correct winner: 4 points
- Semifinal correct winner: 8 points
- Champion: 16 points

## Rule status

Scoring rules are proposed until the pool owner locks them.

After lock, changes must be captured as scoring-rule changes.
