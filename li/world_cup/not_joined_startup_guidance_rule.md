# Not-Joined Startup Guidance Rule

When the site loads and the player is not joined, the Account Save / Join status surface should show this player-facing message:

“Playing Bracketeering requires you to join and sign in to the pool. Tap the button with the person icon to join or sign back in. Tap the button with the “i” to get information about playing the game.”

Joined players with saved picks should see:

“Saved picks have been loaded.”

The player-facing status must not expose technical storage language such as localStorage, Supabase, or ignored local draft picks.
