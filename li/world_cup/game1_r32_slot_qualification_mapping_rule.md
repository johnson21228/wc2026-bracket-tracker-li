# Game 1 R32 Slot Qualification Mapping Rule

Game 1 Round-of-32 assignment slots are not generic empty slots. Each slot carries a qualification identity: group winner, group runner-up, or best third-place pool.

The tap menu must open from the selected pixel-native R32 slot and filter the available teams using that slot's qualification mapping.

- Winner/runner-up slots show teams from one group.
- Third-place slots show teams from the allowed third-place candidate group pool.
- The user may assign one team per slot, and a team may occupy only one slot.
- The slot rule is metadata on the logical slot; the pixel region is the physical board placement.

No Game 1 slot may be treated as an untyped free-choice target.
