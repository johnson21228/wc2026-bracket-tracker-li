# Game 1 projection lifecycle rule

Game 1 begins as a pre-lock Round of 32 projection game and transitions into a standard knockout bracket game after FIFA locks the official Round of 32.

Requirements:

- Pre-lock user projection picks must be preserved as historical scoring evidence.
- Curated standings projection must be separate from player picks.
- Official locked Round of 32 must be separate from both player picks and curated projection.
- Knockout winner picks happen after lock and must not overwrite pre-lock projection evidence.
- The same game board may display all layers, but storage must keep them distinct.
