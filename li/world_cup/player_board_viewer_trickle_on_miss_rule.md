# Player Board Viewer Trickle-On Miss Rule

When official knockout truth resolves an earlier match, a player's downstream pick may become impossible before that downstream slot has its own official result.

This is a **trickle-on miss**.

A direct resolved miss shows the official winner. A trickle-on miss keeps the player's pick visible, but deemphasizes it and labels it `Eliminated`.

This applies to the Pool player board viewer and future read-only player-pick inspection surfaces.
