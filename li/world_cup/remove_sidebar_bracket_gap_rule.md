# Remove Sidebar-Bracket Gap Rule

## Purpose

The team well and bracket should feel like one working surface.

There should not be a large unused horizontal gap between the left team well and the bracket.

## Rule

Use a compact split-screen layout:

```text
narrow team well | bracket surface
```

The bracket should begin directly adjacent to the team well, with only a small gutter.

## UI guidance

Preferred public layout:

- left team well roughly 240–280px wide
- small gap/gutter between panels
- bracket area takes remaining width
- compact team rows
- compact flag-only bracket slots
- no large blank vertical strip between left panel and bracket

## Screenshot rule

Screenshots should show the relationship between source teams and bracket clearly, without wasted horizontal space.
