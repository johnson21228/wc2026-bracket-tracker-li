# Official Truth R32-Only Seed Rule

During the current pre-knockout truth-seed state, site-owned official truth may only contain Round of 32 team occupants.

`site/data/current/official_truth.json` must not prefill winners or later-round result truth for R16, QF, SF, Final, third-place, or Champion slots until those outcomes are official and intentionally entered.

This protects player agency and scoring integrity: player brackets choose winners; official truth only scores against known official outcomes.
