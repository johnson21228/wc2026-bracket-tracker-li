# R32 Pick Card Abbreviation Quiet Typography Rule

The R32 pick card face is a compact board label, not a poster headline.

A filled R32 pick card face must show:

- the selected team's flag
- the selected team's three-letter team abbreviation

The abbreviation must be quiet and readable:

- normal font style
- regular or medium visual weight only
- no bold, black, heavy, poster, or headline weight
- no condensed headline face
- consistent size, weight, style, and letter spacing across every R32 pick card

The board slot rectangle remains the authority. Typography may be tuned only inside the slot rectangle. The card must not grow beyond the slot to accommodate visual style.

The full team name remains in the R32 pick details surface / tooltip, not on the compact card face.
