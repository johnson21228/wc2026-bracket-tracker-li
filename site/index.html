How to play

Welcome to the World Cup Bracketeering Hub.

First, register to join the Pool. Tap the button that looks like a person in the upper-right corner of the screen. Google sign-in is easiest because it helps you avoid having to find a verification email that might land in your spam folder.

You can also choose or edit your player name by pressing that same person/profile button.

After that, you are all set.

Navigate the game board like Google Maps: scroll, zoom, and pan to move around. Then tap or click each bracket slot to pick the winning team.

This is Bracketeering! It is a fun hobby project for me, and I am thrilled if you give it a try.

The winner gets $50.

Your picks will be frozen at 3 PM today, so please pick your bracket ASAP.

Please do not hesitate to text me, Steve, at 410-925-7495 if you have any questions.

Scoring

  • 1 point for each correct Round of 32 winner.
  • 2 points for each correct Round of 16 winner.
  • 4 points for each correct Quarterfinal winner.
  • 8 points for each correct Semifinal winner.
  • 16 points for correctly picking the World Cup champion.

There is no tiebreaker at the moment.

Have fun.
