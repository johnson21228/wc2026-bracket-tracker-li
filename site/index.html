How to play

This is your World Cup Bracketeering Hub. Tap or click each bracket slot and pick a team. This is Bracketeering! This is a hobby project for me, and I am thrilled if you give it a try. The winner gets $50 from me. Please do not hesitate to text Steve at 410-925-7495 if you have any questions.

Navigate the game board like Google Maps: scroll, zoom, and pan to move around, then tap or click to make your picks. If a pick turns red, that means there is a conflict and you need to find a way to resolve it — for example, you can’t assign a team to more than one slot.

Press the join button to play the game with others and have a chance to win the $50 prize. If you join using email, the verification email may end up in your spam/junk folder.

The game has two parts

Part one is about “Group Stage Picks.” Part two is about “Knockout Round Picks.” You do not need to play part one — you can just play part two, the knockout round. Part one results are used as a tiebreaker if there is a tie in part two.

Group Stage Picks

The first part asks you to pick the Round of 32 bracket. Pick the teams that will be in the bracket as FIFA reveals them after group play finishes. Make your picks before 11:59 PM on Thursday to participate in this part.

Group Stage is used as the tiebreaker if there is a tie after the final knockout match on July 19.

Knockout Round Picks

Knockout Stage is the second part of the game. It is the simplest and easiest-to-understand part. Just pick the winners of each knockout game.

Knockout Stage picks are locked when the first knockout match begins.

Scoring

Group Stage: 1 point for each correctly predicted Round-of-32 team. The team must be in the correct bracket slot.

Knockout Stage:

  • 1 point for each correct Round of 32 winner.
  • 2 points for each correct Round of 16 winner.
  • 4 points for each correct Quarterfinal winner.
  • 8 points for each correct Semifinal winner.
  • 16 points for correctly picking the World Cup champion.

Knockout points are awarded when the team you picked wins that match.

Please, just have fun making picks. The group buttons will show you the group standings if you are playing part one.
